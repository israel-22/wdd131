Ecuatorian Flavors

Site Name

Chosen Name:Ecuatorian Flavors

Reason: This name represents the diversity, richness, and cultural identity reflected in Ecuador’s traditional dishes. It highlights the unique flavors that define Ecuadorian cuisine and invites visitors to explore and experience them.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to serve as a guide for people interested in learning about Ecuadorian culture through food. It will provide traditional recipes, background information about typical dishes, and step-by-step instructions for preparing them at home. Additionally, the website will highlight the cultural significance of these dishes, making it useful for tourists, food enthusiasts, and anyone interested in Ecuador.

Scenario

  • “What are the most traditional Ecuadorian dishes and how can I prepare them at home?”
  • “Where can I learn about Ecuador’s food culture and the origins of its most iconic recipes?”

Color Scheme

Primary Color: #006F3C (Green) – Used for headings, accents, and navigation elements.

Secondary Color: #F2B705 (Golden Yellow) – Used for highlights, backgrounds, and decorative accents.
